Dapps and swap

Dapps, short for Decentralized Application, is a software
application that operates on a blockchain or distributed
ledger technology (DLT). Unlike traditional applications,
Dapps are decentralized and have no single point of
control or failure

Hardware Wallet

A hardware wallet is a specialized cryptocurrency wallet
that stores the private keys necessary to access and
manage your cryptocurrency holdings in a physical,
offline device. It is considered one of the most secure
methods for safeguarding your digital assets

Healthcare telemedicine

Telemedicine, also known as telehealth, is a healthcare
delivery model that uses technology to provide medical
services remotely. It has become increasingly important,
especially in light of the COVID-19 pandemic, as a means
to provide healthcare services while minimizing in-
person contact.

AI-Enhanced Healthcare

AI-enhanced healthcare refers to the integration of
artificial intelligence (AI) technologies and techniques
into various aspects of the healthcare industry to
improve patient care, diagnostics, treatment, and
healthcare system operations
